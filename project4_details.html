Water Pump Impeller

What?

  • Create and design an impeller which is able to quickly pump water vertically using a DC Motor power supply.

How?

  • Designed in Autodesk Inventor.
  • Used 3D printing to make the impeller.
  • Used drills to create the necessary product which allowed for the impeller to be used.

Results

  • It took 91 seconds to move a Liter of water vertically 0.5 meters.
  • Reduced the time by 35% compared to the original impeller.
